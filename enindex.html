our house

Our house is located in the Camino Real neighborhood, in the municipality of Zapopan, Jalisco. We wanted the area to be quiet and close to important avenues such as Vallarta Avenue, which connects to the heart of the Metropolitan Zone Of Guadalajara.

We have the best shopping centers just minutes away! We are 5 minutes from La Gran Plaza, 7 minutes from Plaza Galerias and 15 minutes from the exclusive Plaza Andares. La Minerva is 7 minutes away, and we are only 35 minutes from the Guadalajara International Airport.

FACILITIES

We have spaces in which you can relax and enjoy your stay with us, dining room, patio, living rooms.

NUTRITION

Our specialists in nutrition have designed a complete menu, we know that each person has different nutritional needs and that one of the most important care after your surgery for your wounds to heal properly is to receive foods that contain the necessary nutrients to help your body recover and have the result you want.

TRANSPORTATION

We have premium transportation units so you can enjoy a comfortable and safe trip during your stay. Our driver will pick you up and take you to the recovery house.

nurses 24/7

We know how important the recovery period is, that's why we have the best team of certified nurses, dedicated to give you a personalized, super friendly and efficient care. They will help you to bathe, perform your cures and give you your medications. They will be there for you 24 hours a day!

PERSONAL THERAPY

As part of your recovery, it is usually recommended that you undergo postoperative rehabilitation therapies to alleviate pain, help the correct healing process and reduce inflammation of the operated tissues. We have medical staff and certified therapists with extensive experience in providing this type of medical service as part of your recovery experience.
